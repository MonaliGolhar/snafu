  • Infrastructure: This is the most important factor while choosing a college.It includes labs, workshop, computer labs, sufficient classrooms, all the equipments in labs etc.
  • Placements: This may specify your future after four years of engineering. Gather the information about which companies visit the college for placements and statistics of annual placement.
  • Alumni guidance: To know about the college from the person who learnt there helps a lot in understanding the culture of that college.
  • Faculty: Faculty should be well experienced, sufficiently qualified , concerned about students understanding and updated with recent developements in their field.
  • Location and facilities: Location of the college should be convenient for you because it directly affects your daily lifestyle. Facilities like hostel, internet, library, mess should be available as per your requirement.
