  • Talk to your seniors about college,they will give you important and practical information.
  • Eg. they can tell you which one is the better food spot or library is more silent or reading hall etc.

  • Talk to people in your class or hostel mates.
  • You will feel more confident if you have friends.
  • You will not feel homesick.

  • Know about various groups in your college.
  • Be a member of that group you are interested in.
  • Don't join too many groups because it will make you too much busy and you won't get time for studies.
